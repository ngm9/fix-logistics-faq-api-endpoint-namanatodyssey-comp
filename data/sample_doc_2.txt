Logistics FAQ: Returns, Damage, and Claims

Q: How do I return a damaged shipment?
A: If your shipment arrived damaged, photograph the damaged packaging and contents immediately before discarding any materials. Submit a damage claim through our returns portal at returns.shipfast.com within 48 hours of delivery. Attach the photographs and your order ID. Our team will review the claim within 2 business days and issue a replacement or refund depending on item availability.

Q: What is the return window for standard orders?
A: Standard orders may be returned within 30 days of the delivery date. Items must be unused, in their original packaging, and accompanied by the original packing slip. Perishable goods, custom orders, and items marked as final sale are not eligible for return.

Q: How long does a refund take after a return is processed?
A: Once we receive and inspect the returned item, refunds are processed within 3 to 5 business days. The refund will be credited to the original payment method. Depending on your bank or card issuer, it may take an additional 2 to 7 business days for the credit to appear on your statement.

Q: My order is missing items. What should I do?
A: If your order arrived with missing items, first check the packing slip included in the box to confirm all listed items were supposed to be included in that shipment. Some orders ship in multiple packages. If an item listed on your packing slip is genuinely missing, contact support within 7 days of delivery with your order ID and the packing slip. We will arrange a replacement shipment at no additional cost.

Q: Can I refuse a delivery if the package looks damaged?
A: Yes. If the outer packaging shows clear signs of significant damage such as crushed corners, punctures, or wet damage at the time of delivery, you have the right to refuse acceptance. Ask the delivery driver to note the refusal and the reason. Contact our support team the same day with your tracking number so we can process a replacement order promptly.
